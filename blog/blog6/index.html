  • Step 2: After entering the command, you should be able to see the following feedback. Enter the link and go to the streamlit dashboard page.

  • Steamlit Dashboard Description:

  • This is table 1 in the dashboard. As shown by the plot, the number of awarded PhDs is stably increasing with time.

  • You can also check the box to take a look at raw data used to make the plot.

  • This is table 4 in the dashboard. The universities with most number of awarded PhDs are indicated by the plot. Currently the subject is “Life Science”. This means that these universities have strength in this discipline.
